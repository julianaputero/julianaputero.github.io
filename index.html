Córdoba, Argentina

I am PhD. Student in Computer Science and a member of the Logics Interaction and Inteligent System Group (LIIS). My supervisors are Raul Fervari and Valentin Cassano

I obtained my UGrad degree in Mathematics in 2021 from the University of Rio Cuarto (UNRC).

In 2022 I obtained a full-time CONICET scholarship to pursue my PhD. studies in Computer Science at the Faculty of Mathematics, Astronomy, Physics, and Computing (FaMAF) at the Univerity of Cordoba, Argentina. My expected graduation date is in 2027.

My PhD. is in the field of Computational Logics and, more precisly, in the area of Modal Logics. The title of my PhD. research proposal is Description Logics over Data Graphs.

My research interests include:

Modal Logics. Description Logics.
